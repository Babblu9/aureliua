The Aurelius Offerings

Aurelius Global Scholars

A comprehensive pathway for students aspiring to leading universities in the United States, United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, Singapore, Europe, and beyond.

Overview

Built for Ambitious Students Who Want the World

Aurelius Global Scholars is our flagship pathway for students who see their future at the world's most selective universities. It is not a coaching pathway — it is a multi-year journey of academic preparation, intellectual exploration, leadership development, and admissions strategy.

Students work with mentors who understand the expectations of top universities in the US, UK, Canada, Australia, Singapore, and Europe. Every element of the pathway — from SAT preparation to essay development — is connected to a single goal: helping each student present a distinctive, authentic, and compelling application.

Whether a student joins in Grade 9 to build a long-term profile or enters a more intensive cycle in Grade 11, the pathway adapts to their timeline, strengths, and university ambitions.

Target Destinations

US, UK, Canada, Australia, Singapore, Europe, and other leading global higher education systems.

Ideal For

Students in Grades 9–12 who aspire to study at top-ranked international universities.

Outcome

A well-rounded scholar with strong test scores, a distinctive profile, and a compelling university application.

What the Pathway Includes

Every Element of a Strong Application

SAT Preparation

Comprehensive training in reading, writing, and mathematics with full-length practice tests, performance analytics, and personalised feedback to maximise scores.

IELTS Preparation

Structured coaching across listening, reading, writing, and speaking to achieve the band scores required by leading universities worldwide.

AP Exam Preparation

Subject-specific Advanced Placement support that strengthens academic depth and demonstrates readiness for university-level work.

Academic Enrichment

Advanced reading, writing, research, and critical thinking modules that go beyond the school curriculum and prepare students for rigorous academics.

Research & Independent Projects

Guidance on designing and executing independent research, capstone projects, and academic explorations that demonstrate intellectual curiosity.

Leadership Development

Mentorship to identify meaningful leadership opportunities, build teams, take initiative, and create measurable impact in school or community.

Profile Building

Strategic planning of extracurricular activities, competitions, internships, and summer programmes aligned with academic interests.

Essay Development

Personal statement and supplemental essay coaching that helps students find their authentic voice and craft memorable narratives.

Interview Preparation

Mock interviews, feedback sessions, and communication coaching for Oxbridge, Ivy League, alumni, and scholarship interviews.

University Selection

Research-driven shortlisting of reach, match, and safety universities based on academic fit, career goals, and admissions probability.

Scholarship Guidance

Identification of merit-based and need-based scholarship opportunities, plus support for applications and financial documentation.

Admissions Strategy

End-to-end planning of timelines, recommendation letters, application portals, and final decisions across multiple countries.

Pathway Formats

Choose the Structure That Fits Your Journey

Integrated Pathway

A multi-year pathway combining intermediate academics with SAT preparation, AP coursework, enrichment, and full admissions support. Designed for students who want a structured, long-term approach.

  • Intermediate + SAT
  • AP Preparation
  • Profile Building
  • Full Admissions Support

Evening Scholars Pathway

A flexible after-school pathway for students who attend regular school during the day and want focused preparation for global university admissions in the evenings.

  • Evening Sessions
  • Test Prep Focus
  • Weekend Workshops
  • Application Guidance
