Selection Test

AuSAT
Aurelius Scholastic Aptitude Test

AuSAT is the official student assessment of Aurelius Academy, designed to evaluate aptitude, reasoning ability, academic readiness, and learning potential.

The assessment helps students better understand their strengths and enables Aurelius mentors to recommend the most appropriate academic pathways, preparation programs, and university admissions strategies.

Whether a student is exploring SAT, IELTS, AP Exams, IPMAT, profile building, or international admissions opportunities, AuSAT serves as the starting point for a personalized Aurelius journey.
