Our flagship two-year integrated program combining Intermediate education with preparation for global university admissions.   • Intermediate Education

  • AP Examinations Guidance
  • Profile Development
  • University Admissions Guidance
  • Academic & Mentorship Support

Comprehensive Student Assessment
AuSAT
Aurelius Scholastic Aptitude Test

AuSAT is Aurelius Academy's comprehensive student assessment, designed to identify each student's academic strengths, learning potential, and readiness for future success.

Through a balanced evaluation of aptitude, reasoning, academic readiness, and critical thinking, AuSAT provides meaningful insights that help students and parents make informed academic decisions.

Every participant receives a comprehensive Aurelius Report, offering detailed performance insights, strengths analysis, and personalised academic pathway recommendations.
