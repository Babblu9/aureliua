The Aurelius Offerings

One Philosophy.
Endless Possibilities.

Discover thoughtfully designed programs that prepare students for admission to leading universities worldwide.

Aurelius Scholars

A focused preparation program for students aspiring to undergraduate education at leading universities worldwide.

Includes
  • SAT Preparation
  • IELTS Preparation
  • Diagnostic Assessments
  • Full-Length Mock Tests
  • Performance Analysis
  • Strategy Sessions
Aurelius Signature

Our flagship university admissions program, providing comprehensive guidance from academic preparation to successful university applications.

Includes
  • Everything in Aurelius Scholars
  • Profile Building
  • University Shortlisting
  • Application Strategy
  • Personal Statement & Essay Guidance
  • Admissions Counselling
  • Interview Preparation (where applicable)
Aurelius AP Excellence

A specialized program for students preparing for Advanced Placement (AP) examinations, combining conceptual learning, rigorous subject preparation, and structured practice.

Includes
  • Subject-Specific AP Preparation
  • Conceptual Learning
  • Practice Assessments
  • Performance Analysis
  • Exam Strategy Sessions
  • Academic Mentoring
Aurelius Global

Our flagship two-year integrated program combining Intermediate education with preparation for global university admissions. Students benefit from a seamless academic journey that brings together school education, standardized test preparation, profile development, and admissions guidance within a single, structured learning experience.

Includes
  • Intermediate Education
  • SAT & IELTS Preparation
  • AP Examinations Guidance
  • Profile Development
  • University Admissions Guidance
  • Academic & Mentorship Support
