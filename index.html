A Personal Note

From the Founder's Desk.

"We do not simply prepare students for examinations. We prepare them for the lives those examinations make possible."

At Aurelius Academy of Global Studies, we believe education should extend far beyond examination success. We exist to cultivate intellectual curiosity, academic excellence, and the confidence to thrive in an increasingly interconnected world.

Too often, students receive information when what they truly need is thoughtful guidance. At Aurelius, we combine rigorous academic preparation with personalised mentorship, helping each student discover opportunities, make informed decisions, and pursue ambitious goals with clarity and purpose.

Whether a student’s aspirations lead to leading universities in India or across the world, our commitment remains the same: to provide the preparation, perspective, and support that transform potential into meaningful achievement.

We believe talent is universal, but opportunity is not. Through high standards, individual attention, and a global outlook, we strive to ensure that every deserving student has access to the guidance and opportunities needed to realise their full potential.

Every scholar’s journey is unique. We are honoured to be part of that journey.

Anil Nekkalapudi
Founder & Chief Vision Architect
B.E. (Hons.), BITS Pilani

Our Advisory Approach

Finding the Right Fit.

Every student brings a unique combination of strengths, aspirations, and potential. Our advisory process is designed to identify the universities, programs, and pathways where each scholar can thrive academically and personally.

Discover

We begin with a comprehensive understanding of the student's academic profile, interests, aspirations, and long-term goals.

Shortlist

We develop a carefully balanced university list that aligns ambition with opportunity, combining aspirational, matched, and secure options.

Prepare

From standardized tests and academic enrichment to essays and profile development, every element is strategically aligned with the expectations of target universities.

Apply

We provide end-to-end guidance through applications, interviews, deadlines, and decision-making, ensuring every submission reflects the student's strongest potential.
